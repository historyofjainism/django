About

It’s time the ‘real Jainism history’ is told.

Jain Dharm is eternal. It is important to reclaim our history and write it from our ‘perspective’. Kings like Kumarpal, Shrenik, great ministers like Vastupal, Tejpal and great Jain Acharyas like Vajraswami, Hemachandracharya do not find prominent place in the history books taught in school. "History of Jainism" portal has undertaken objective of presenting ancient and glorious history of Jainism.The History is the mirror of the past.
